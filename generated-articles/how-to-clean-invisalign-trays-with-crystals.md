## Step-by-Step Guide to Cleaning Invisalign Trays With Crystals

Now that you know which crystals to use, let’s dive into the cleaning process. This method is simple and requires minimal effort.

1.  **Gather Your Supplies:** You’ll need your chosen crystals, a small bowl, distilled water, and a soft-bristled brush (like a baby toothbrush).
2.  **Cleanse the Crystals:** Before using them, cleanse your crystals under running water or with sage smoke to remove any accumulated energy.
3.  **Create a Crystal Infusion:** Place the crystals in the bowl and fill it with distilled water. Let the crystals infuse the water for at least 30 minutes, or even overnight for a deeper clean.
4.  **Soak Your Trays:** Remove your Invisalign trays and place them in the crystal-infused water. Ensure the trays are fully submerged.
5.  **Gentle Scrubbing:** After soaking for 15-20 minutes, use the soft-bristled brush to gently scrub the trays, paying attention to any areas with visible buildup.
6.  **Rinse Thoroughly:** Rinse the trays thoroughly under cool, running distilled water to remove any remaining crystal residue.
7.  **Dry and Store:** Allow the trays to air dry completely before placing them back in your mouth.

## Preventing Discoloration and Odor in Your Invisalign Trays

Cleaning is only half the battle. Preventing discoloration and odor is equally important. Here are some tips:

*   **Remove Trays While Eating and Drinking:** Always remove your trays before eating or drinking anything other than water.
*   **Rinse After Every Meal:** Rinse your trays with water immediately after removing them to prevent food particles from drying and staining.
*   **Store Properly:** When not in use, store your trays in their case to protect them from bacteria and damage.
*   **Regular Professional Cleaning:** Consider getting your trays professionally cleaned by your orthodontist periodically.
*   **Hydrate Frequently:** Drinking plenty of water helps keep your mouth clean and prevents dry mouth, which can contribute to odor.

## Frequently Asked Questions About Cleaning Invisalign With Crystals

**Q: Is it safe to use any crystal for cleaning my Invisalign trays?**

A: No, it’s not. Avoid using porous or easily dissolvable crystals, as they could damage your trays. Stick to hard, non-reactive crystals like clear quartz, amethyst, and rose quartz.

**Q: How often should I clean my Invisalign trays with crystals?**

A: You should clean your trays with crystals at least once a week, or more frequently if you notice any discoloration or buildup.

**Q: Can I use tap water instead of distilled water?**

A: It’s best to use distilled water, as tap water can contain minerals and chemicals that could leave a residue on your trays.

**Q: Will crystals scratch my Invisalign trays?**

A: No, crystals are generally gentle enough not to scratch your trays, especially when used with a soft-bristled brush. However, avoid applying excessive pressure.

**Q: Can I use essential oils with the crystal cleaning solution?**

A: While some essential oils have antibacterial properties, it’s best to avoid them as they could potentially damage the plastic of your Invisalign trays.
