Are you looking to effectively **clean hard water stains from your stainless steel sink**? Hard water deposits can make even the cleanest kitchen look dull and neglected. These unsightly mineral spots often appear as white, chalky residue, or even stubborn grayish patches on your pristine stainless steel surfaces.
