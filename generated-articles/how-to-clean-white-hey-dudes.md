## Keeping Your Kicks Bright: How to Clean White Hey Dudes

Let's face it: white shoes look fantastic, but they also attract dirt like magnets.  If you love your white Hey Dudes, you know the struggle of keeping them looking pristine.  This article will walk you through everything you need to know to effectively clean your white Hey Dudes and keep them looking their best. We’ll cover everything from quick spot cleaning to more thorough washes, ensuring your favorite shoes stay fresh and stylish.

**Takeaway:**

*   Use a mild detergent and soft brush for regular cleaning.
*   Remove insoles for separate washing and faster drying.
*   Air dry your Hey Dudes, avoiding direct sunlight or heat.
*   Protect your clean shoes with a fabric protector spray.

**Quick Answer:**

To clean white Hey Dudes, mix a small amount of mild detergent with lukewarm water. Gently scrub the shoes with a soft brush, rinse thoroughly, and air dry. Removing the insoles speeds up the drying process and helps prevent odors.

## Understanding Your Hey Dudes: Materials & Care

Hey Dudes are known for their comfort and casual style, but understanding their materials is key to cleaning them properly. Most Hey Dudes feature a canvas or cotton upper, a flexible rubber sole, and a removable memory foam insole.  Knowing this helps you choose the right cleaning methods.  Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners, as these can damage the fabric and discolor the shoes.  Gentle is always best when it comes to maintaining the quality of your Hey Dudes.

## Simple Spot Cleaning for Everyday Dirt

Sometimes, a full wash isn’t necessary. For small marks or everyday dirt, spot cleaning is a quick and effective solution.  Start by mixing a tiny amount of mild laundry detergent with cool water. Dip a clean cloth or soft-bristled brush into the solution and gently blot the affected area.  Avoid rubbing vigorously, as this can spread the stain.  Follow up with a clean, damp cloth to remove any soap residue, and let the area air dry. ### Dealing with Mud and Grass Stains

Mud and grass stains require a little extra attention.  Let the mud dry completely before attempting to remove it. Once dry, gently brush off as much dried mud as possible with a soft brush.  Then, follow the spot cleaning method described above, using a slightly stronger concentration of detergent if needed. For stubborn grass stains, you might consider a paste of baking soda and water, applying it to the stain and letting it sit for 30 minutes before rinsing.

## Deep Cleaning Your White Hey Dudes: A Step-by-Step Guide

For a more thorough clean, you can hand wash your white Hey Dudes.  First, remove the insoles and laces. These can be washed separately (more on that later!).  Fill a basin or sink with lukewarm water and add a small amount of mild laundry detergent.  Gently submerge the shoes and use a soft-bristled brush to scrub the canvas upper and rubber sole.  Pay attention to areas that are particularly dirty. Rinse the shoes thoroughly with clean water, ensuring all soap residue is removed.  [https://beacleaner.com/how-to-clean-dudes](https://beacleaner.com/how-to-clean-dudes) provides a broader overview of cleaning different types of Hey Dudes.

### Washing the Insoles and Laces

Don't forget the insoles and laces!  Hand wash the insoles with the same mild detergent and water solution.  Rinse them thoroughly and allow them to air dry completely.  For laces, you can either hand wash them or place them in a laundry bag and wash them in the washing machine on a gentle cycle.  Replacing the insoles periodically can also help keep your Hey Dudes fresh and comfortable.

## Drying Your Hey Dudes Properly

Proper drying is crucial to prevent damage and maintain the shape of your Hey Dudes.  **Never** put your Hey Dudes in the dryer! The heat can shrink the canvas and damage the glue.  Instead, stuff the shoes with clean paper towels or newspaper to help them retain their shape and absorb excess moisture.  Place them in a well-ventilated area to air dry, away from direct sunlight or heat.  Direct sunlight can cause the white canvas to yellow over time.  Allow at least 24 hours for the shoes to dry completely.

## Protecting Your Clean Hey Dudes

Once your Hey Dudes are clean and dry, consider protecting them from future stains and dirt.  A fabric protector spray designed for canvas shoes can create a barrier that repels water and stains.  Apply the spray evenly over the entire shoe, following the manufacturer's instructions.  This will make future cleaning much easier and help your white Hey Dudes stay looking their best for longer. ## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q: Can I use bleach to clean my white Hey Dudes?**

A: No, avoid using bleach. Bleach can damage the canvas and cause discoloration. It's best to stick to mild detergents and gentle cleaning methods.

**Q: How often should I clean my Hey Dudes?**

A: It depends on how often you wear them and the conditions you wear them in. Spot clean as needed, and deep clean every few months or when they become visibly dirty.

**Q: What if my Hey Dudes still smell after cleaning?**

A: Sprinkle baking soda inside the shoes and let it sit overnight. Baking soda absorbs odors. Vacuum out the baking soda the next day.

**Q: Can I put my Hey Dudes in the washing machine?**

A: While some people do, it's generally not recommended. The washing machine can be too harsh and damage the shoes. Hand washing is the safest option.

**Q: How do I remove scuff marks from the rubber sole?**

A: A magic eraser can often remove scuff marks from the rubber sole. Gently rub the scuff marks with the eraser until they disappear.
