## Prepare Your White Hey Dudes for Cleaning

Starting clean means a better final result. Before any wet cleaning, you must prepare your shoes. This step is important for getting rid of loose dirt and debris. It also helps protect parts of your shoes that might get damaged by water. Taking a few minutes now saves you effort later.

Begin by removing the shoelaces from your Hey Dudes. These often collect dirt and can be cleaned separately. Pull out the insoles too, if they are removable. Insoles can hold odors and dirt. You can wash them by hand or wipe them down with a damp cloth. A clean insole makes the whole shoe feel fresh.

Next, grab a dry, soft-bristled brush. An old toothbrush works well for this. Gently brush off any loose dirt, mud, or dust from the canvas or knit parts of your shoes. Pay attention to the soles too. Use the brush to scrub away any trapped dirt in the grooves. This dry removal stops dirt from turning into muddy smears when wet.

*   **Remove Laces:** Untie and pull out all shoelaces.
*   **Remove Insoles:** Take out the insoles if they come out easily.
*   **Dry Brush:** Use a soft brush to remove loose dirt from the fabric and soles.
*   **Tap Out Debris:** Gently tap your shoes together to shake out any loose debris from inside.

This preparation step makes the cleaning process much easier. It ensures you tackle only the embedded dirt, not just surface grime.

## Spot Cleaning Small Marks on White Hey Dudes

White Hey Dudes can get small marks easily. Daily wear often causes light scuffs or dirt spots. Spot cleaning is the fastest way to deal with these. It stops small stains from becoming bigger problems. This method is also good for shoes that do not need a full wash. You can do this quickly after each wear to keep your shoes looking good.

To start, mix a small amount of mild liquid detergent with water. A teaspoon of dish soap in a cup of water works well. You want a soapy solution, not thick suds. Dip a clean cloth or a soft brush into this mix. Gently rub the stained area. Use small circles and light pressure. Do not rub too hard, as this can spread the stain or damage the fabric.

For tougher marks on the rubber soles, a magic eraser works wonders. Wet the eraser and rub it along the rubber parts. It will remove scuffs and dark lines. Remember to only use it on the rubber, not the fabric. This tool can make your soles look new again. For more general advice on cleaning rubber parts, see our guide on [how to clean white rubber shoes](https://homeessentialsguide.com/how-to-clean-white-rubber-shoes).

After cleaning the spot, use a clean, damp cloth to wipe away any soap rest. You do not want soap residue left on the fabric. It can attract more dirt or even cause new stains if it dries on the shoe. Let the spot air dry completely before wearing the shoes again. This method helps maintain your Hey Dudes' brightness without a full wash.

*   **Prepare Solution:** Mix mild soap with water.
*   **Apply and Scrub:** Use a cloth or brush to gently clean the stain.
*   **Clean Rubber:** Use a magic eraser for scuffs on soles.
*   **Rinse Area:** Wipe with a clean, damp cloth.
*   **Air Dry:** Allow the treated area to dry completely.

## Deep Cleaning Your White Hey Dudes: Hand Washing Method

Sometimes, spot cleaning is not enough. Your white Hey Dudes might need a full, deep clean. Hand washing is the safest way to clean your shoes thoroughly. It gives you full control over the process. This method helps protect the shoe fabric and shape. It is a gentle way to remove dirt that has set in. Many shoe care experts recommend hand washing over machine washing for delicate items.

First, fill a basin or tub with cool or lukewarm water. Add a small amount of gentle liquid detergent. Choose a soap free from harsh chemicals or bleach. Bleach can damage fabric or cause yellowing. You want enough soap to create some suds, but not too much. A gentle laundry detergent or even mild dish soap is suitable. For a deep clean that protects fabric, a delicate soap works best.

Submerge your Hey Dudes in the soapy water. Use a soft brush, like an old toothbrush or a nail brush, to scrub the entire shoe. Focus on areas with heavy dirt or stains. Pay attention to the seams and around the eyelets where dirt can hide. Scrub the fabric gently but firmly. Do not forget the inside of the shoes. You can clean the inside fabric and footbed with the same soapy water. If you need more general advice on how to clean white canvas shoes, you can find helpful tips in our guide on [how to clean all white converse](https://homeessentialsguide.com/how-to-clean-all-white-converse).

After scrubbing, rinse the shoes thoroughly under cool, running water. Make sure all soap residue is gone. Squeeze out excess water gently. Do not twist or wring the shoes, as this can distort their shape. This careful approach ensures your Hey Dudes stay clean and keep their original form.

*   **Prepare Water:** Fill a basin with cool water and gentle detergent.
*   **Submerge and Scrub:** Place shoes in water and scrub with a soft brush.
*   **Rinse Thoroughly:** Rinse under cool running water until all soap is gone.
*   **Remove Excess Water:** Gently press out water without wringing.

## Machine Washing White Hey Dudes Safely

Machine washing offers a convenient way to clean your Hey Dudes. However, it requires careful steps to protect your shoes. Not all Hey Dude models are machine washable. Check the care label on your specific pair first. If you choose this method, be sure to follow proper procedures. Machine washing can save time, but improper use can cause damage. Always prioritize the shoe's material and construction.

Before placing your Hey Dudes in the washing machine, take out the laces and insoles. Clean them separately. Put the shoes, laces, and insoles into a mesh laundry bag. This bag protects them from getting tangled or banging around during the wash cycle. It also prevents small parts from getting lost in the machine. A laundry bag is a simple tool that offers great protection.

Use a small amount of mild, bleach-free detergent. Choose a liquid detergent over powder to avoid residue. Set your washing machine to a delicate or gentle cycle. Always use cold water. Hot water can shrink the fabric or damage the glue. Avoid using fabric softener, as it can leave a film on the shoes. A cold, gentle wash is key to cleaning without harm. For a broader understanding of how to wash shoes in a machine, you can read our detailed guide on [how to clean shoes in the washer](https://homeessentialsguide.com/how-to-clean-shoes-in-the-washer).

After the cycle finishes, take your Hey Dudes out immediately. Do not leave them sitting in the machine. This can lead to mildew or odors. Prepare them for air drying right away. A safe machine wash leaves your shoes clean without causing wear.

*   **Check Label:** Ensure your Hey Dudes are machine washable.
*   **Prepare Shoes:** Remove laces and insoles; place all in a laundry bag.
*   **Use Gentle Settings:** Wash on delicate cycle with cold water and mild detergent.
*   **Remove Promptly:** Take shoes out of the washer as soon as the cycle ends.

## Tackling Tough Stains: Specific Solutions for White Hey Dudes

White shoes often pick up stubborn stains. Mud, grass, and grease can be hard to remove. Yellowing can also appear over time. These tough marks need special attention beyond general cleaning. Knowing the right approach for each type of stain helps restore your shoes. Do not give up on a stain before trying a specific solution.

### Removing Mud and Dirt Stains

Let mud dry completely before you try to clean it. Dried mud is easier to brush off. Once dry, use a stiff brush to remove as much as possible. Then, create a paste with baking soda and a little water. Apply the paste directly to the mud stain. Let it sit for 15-20 minutes. Scrub gently with a brush, then rinse. This method lifts the stain from the fabric. For more detailed instructions on removing mud, you can refer to our guide on [how to clean mud off white shoes](https://homeessentialsguide.com/how-to-clean-mud-off-white-shoes).

### Treating Grass and Oil Stains

Grass stains can be tough because of chlorophyll. Rubbing alcohol or a dab of white vinegar can help break down grass stains. Apply a small amount to a clean cloth and blot the stain. Do not rub, as this can spread it. For oil or grease stains, sprinkle cornstarch or talcum powder on the fresh stain. Let it sit for a few hours to absorb the oil. Then brush off the powder and proceed with a general soap and water clean. These absorbents pull the oil out of the fabric.

### Battling Yellowing and Brightening White Shoes

White shoes can turn yellow over time. This happens due to sun exposure, detergents, or age. Hydrogen peroxide is a good solution for yellowing. Mix one part hydrogen peroxide with one part water. Apply the solution to the yellowed areas with a cloth or spray bottle. Let the shoes sit in indirect sunlight for a few hours. The sun activates the peroxide to whiten the fabric. Rinse thoroughly after treatment. Be careful not to use full-strength peroxide, as it can damage fabric over time. Our article on [how to clean white shoes with hydrogen peroxide](https://homeessentialsguide.com/how-to-clean-white-shoes-with-hydrogen-peroxide) provides more details. Alternatively, a paste of baking soda and a small amount of white vinegar can also help lift yellowing. Apply, let dry, then brush off.

*   **Dry Mud First:** Let mud stains dry before brushing and treating.
*   **Baking Soda Paste:** Use for mud and general brightening.
*   **Alcohol/Vinegar:** Good for grass stains.
*   **Absorb Oil:** Use cornstarch or powder for oil stains.
*   **Hydrogen Peroxide Mix:** Effective for yellowing when used with sunlight.

## Drying Your White Hey Dudes Correctly

The drying process is as important as the cleaning itself. Improper drying can lead to several problems. Your shoes might shrink, get a strange shape, or even turn yellow. This is especially true for white shoes. Air drying is always the best method for Hey Dudes. Avoid direct heat at all costs. Following the right steps ensures your shoes dry evenly and keep their original look.

After washing, gently press out as much excess water as you can. Do not twist or wring the shoes. This can damage the fabric and stitching. You want them damp, not dripping wet. The goal is to remove water without harming the shoe's structure. Take your time with this step to avoid stretching or misshaping.

Next, stuff your Hey Dudes with clean paper towels or white paper. Avoid using newspaper, as the ink can transfer and stain your white shoes. The paper helps absorb remaining moisture from the inside. It also helps the shoes keep their shape as they dry. Change the paper every few hours if it becomes very damp. This keeps the drying process moving along.

Place your shoes in a well-ventilated area. Choose a spot away from direct sunlight or heat sources. Sunlight can cause white fabric to yellow. High heat from dryers or radiators can shrink the material or break down glues. An indoor spot with good air flow is perfect. A fan can speed up drying. Let your shoes dry completely, which can take 24-48 hours. Make sure they are totally dry inside before you wear them again to prevent odors or mildew.

*   **Gently Remove Water:** Squeeze excess water without twisting.
*   **Stuff with Paper:** Use white paper towels to absorb moisture and maintain shape.
*   **Air Dry:** Place in a well-ventilated area, away from direct sun or heat.
*   **Allow Full Dry:** Ensure shoes are completely dry before use.

## FAQ Section

### Can I put my white Hey Dudes in the washing machine?
Yes, most white Hey Dudes can go in the washing machine. Always use a mesh laundry bag. Wash them on a delicate or gentle cycle with cold water. Use a mild, bleach-free detergent. This helps protect the fabric and shoe shape.

### How often should I clean my white Hey Dudes?
The frequency depends on how often you wear them and where. If you wear them daily, spot clean as needed and deep clean every 2-4 weeks. For occasional wear, deep clean every 1-2 months. Regular maintenance like dry brushing helps extend time between washes.

### What household products can I use to clean white Hey Dudes?
You can use mild dish soap, baking soda, and white vinegar. A paste of baking soda and water works for stains. A mix of hydrogen peroxide and water can help with yellowing. Always test a small, hidden area first to avoid damage.

### How do I prevent my white Hey Dudes from turning yellow?
Prevent yellowing by air drying in a well-ventilated area away from direct sunlight. Do not use harsh bleaches. Rinse thoroughly after washing to remove all soap residue. Using a mix of hydrogen peroxide and water can help reverse existing yellowing.

### Can I put Hey Dudes in the dryer?
No, do not put Hey Dudes in the dryer. High heat can shrink the fabric, damage the glue, and cause the shoes to lose their shape. Always air dry your Hey Dudes. Stuff them with paper towels to speed up the drying process and help maintain their form.

### How do I clean the rubber soles of my white Hey Dudes?
You can use a magic eraser to clean the rubber soles. Gently rub the eraser on scuffs and dirt marks. A mixture of baking soda and water or a mild soap solution with a brush also works well. Rinse the rubber parts clean with a damp cloth.
