## Restore Your Marble's Shine: How To Clean Stains Effectively

Marble surfaces add timeless elegance to any home. Its luxurious appearance makes it a popular choice for countertops, floors, and decorative pieces. However, marble is a porous natural stone. This means it can easily absorb spills and develop unsightly stains. Knowing how to clean stains on marble is essential for maintaining its beauty. This comprehensive guide will show you specific techniques to remove various types of marble stains. We will cover everything from identifying the stain to applying specialized treatments. You will learn to protect your investment and keep your marble looking new.
