## Clean Resin Off Tools: Your Complete Guide

Working with resin is a rewarding craft, creating beautiful and durable items. However, sticky tools are a common challenge for anyone involved in resin art or construction projects. Resin can cling to everything, from mixing sticks and spreaders to rollers and molds. I have faced this messy situation many times in my own projects. Cleaning resin off tools quickly and effectively keeps your workspace tidy and your equipment ready for the next project. Ignoring resin buildup damages your tools over time.
